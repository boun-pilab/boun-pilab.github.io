More Resources



The BOUN Treebank is a new dependency treebank for Turkish annotated manually. It contains 9757 sentences from various genres.

Here you can find the re-annotated version of the IMST-UD Treebank. The revisions made on the IMST-UD treebank are discussed in our paper published in the proceedings of UDW within SyntaxFest'19.

Under the guidelines directory of the BIMST Treebank page, you can find our new proposed guidelines to the guidelines that can be found in the UD website.

And here, you can find the re-annotated version of the the Turkish PUD Treebank. We discussed the revisions that we made to this treebank in our paper published in the proceedings of ACL LAW XIII.

We also present an annotation tool for dependency parsing, BoAT. You can find the implementation of the BoAT tool in this repository. BoAT annotates the CoNLL-U files easily for dependency parsing. It also visualizes the parse trees.
